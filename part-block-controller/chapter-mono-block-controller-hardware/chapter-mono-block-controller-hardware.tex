\unnumberedChapter{Mono Block Controller}


For some use cases, a quad controller is perhaps a bit too much. How about a block controller with just one channel but more amperage output. A nice feature of the L6205 dual bridge chip is that it allows to connect the two bridges in parallel delivering 5.6 instead of two times 2.8 Ampere. Let's design such a dual block controller board. Setting jumpers on the board will configure a mono block controller with 5.6 Amps or a dial block controller with two times 2.8 Amps. This way we also have a nice booster unit for larger scales.
